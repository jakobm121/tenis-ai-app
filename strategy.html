Your bankroll is the total amount of money you have set aside specifically for sports betting. It‚Äôs crucial that your bankroll is separate from your everyday finances, so betting does not put your personal budget at risk.

a) Determine Your Bet Size

A common method is the fixed percentage of your bankroll. Recommended bet size is usually 1‚Äì5% of your bankroll per wager, depending on risk and confidence.

b) Don‚Äôt Risk Too Much at Once

3. Common Betting Strategies
